Take a look at the example scene:
Scenes/Camera360.unity

You can click the screen and the FollowBox will move to your
cursor position (in world space). The GUI label displays its
screen position.


Scripts/Camera360.cs
This script sets up a Camera array for a 360 degree view.

Scripts/CameraRectLog.cs
This script displays the screen position of a GameObject
in the world.

Scripts/FollowTouch360.cs
This script moves the object it's on to the cursor position
when you click and hold on the screen.
